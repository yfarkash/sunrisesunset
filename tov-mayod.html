    Tov Mayod!

    April 29, 2009

    I started taking a Hebrew class on Monday nights at the JCC (Jewish Community Centre) which is less than a five minute walk from our place.  My teacher always says 'Tov Mayod' to everyone which is very encouraging!  So far we have reviewed the alef-beit, read through a telephone conversation (about a woman named Rivka and her silly Doda Sarah and Dod Yakov), listened to two songs (one was a very depressing song about love gone bad) and written a little story of an argument to act out with a partner.  My story (completed with my classmate Howard) was about a husband being rude to his wife:  (Howard's idea!)

    Husband: 'Ayfo cafe?'

    Wife: 'Sleeyha?'

    Husband: 'Boyee hana.'

    Wife: 'Beh Ahmet??'

    Husband: 'Beh Ahmet!'

    Wife: 'Yish Kasef?'

    Husband: 'Ayn Kasef. .......  Sleeyha.  Ma schlomaha?'

    Wife: 'Lech mi po!'

    Everyone thought this was a very funny story.  Our teacher helped with the final line for the wife because neither Howard nor I knew how to say 'get out!' or anything like that. :)  Here are some pictures of the building where I'm taking my class and of me getting ready to go to class.
