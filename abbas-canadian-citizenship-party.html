    Abba's Canadian Citizenship Party

    January 4, 2020

    Abba became a citizen of Canada in January 3, 2020... and that night, we partied! House party is course - the theme, all things Canadian!

    We had lots of friends over, played Canadian tunes, and ate... poutine of course!

    The poutine was a hit... I knew Yoav got that deep fryer for a reason!

    Even the kids had a ball. We kept them busy!

    These maple leaves were used to create a mural from friends... Their favourite places in Canada, their favourite music, what they find funny or cool about Canada, and well wishes for Yoav...

    All in all, it was an amazing and memorable day for Yoav and our family 💗
