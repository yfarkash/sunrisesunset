    January 3rd, 2020: Abba is a Canadian Citizen!

    January 3, 2020

    Hooray! After years of waiting, Abba became a Canadian citizen in January, 2020!

(We almost didn't get in because we didn't show up in time to get seating... But they found spots for all of us!)

    The ceremony was touching and the judge officiating was warm and inspiring. It was a very happy and touching ceremony.
