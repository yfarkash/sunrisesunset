Sunrise, Sunset

    Shabbat dinner

    February 16, 2009

    OK, seems like this will be a trend, this blogging thing. Last Friday (Feb 13) Denise and I went for Shabbat dinner at Daniel and Terri's. Daniel is a good friend of Denise and Terri is his newest acquisition in the form of a wife. They are great hosts who always have a positive spirit around them. No wonder, since Daniel is an organic farmer and Terri is a drummer (not a rock drummer, though. More of a tribal-style drummer). With us were a few of their friends: one singer, an art teacher, another musical guy, a neurologist (who works with Danny in the same hospital!) and his wife and baby. It was a real fun Kiddush with great vegetarian food which went into the wee hours with drumming, playing guitar and singing. I don't remember the last time Denise was up until 3 in the morning.
