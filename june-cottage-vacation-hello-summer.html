    June Cottage Vacation: Hello Summer

    July 3, 2020

    We got a great Air Bnb in Marmora, Ontario to enjoy for a week at the end of school.

    It was a super fun week of beach time, hiking, biking, fishing, canoeing, paddle boating, campfires, and enjoying our dock and river.

    Some things we'll remember...

    Jacob's wrist got fractured when he fell off his bike before we left. He got a big plaster cast. Like 2 days into vacation we got it wet and had to get it redone.

    We visited Petroglyphs Provincial Park and saw the images carved into rock that create the Native Canadian sacred space. (No pictures.)

    Jacob loved fishing and did lots every day. When we got home to Hamilton and he woke up in the morning, he said 'I wish I could go fishing every morning!' Benji was more into watching TV and YouTube videos about Minecraft.

    We followed a hot tip and stopped at Muddy's Pit for meat on the way home. It was lined up and Benjamin was none too happy waiting. It was worth it for the meat!

    We'd love to go back to this place if we can someday!! It was a great adventure.
