    Science Rendezvous

    May 18, 2009

    Hi,

    Sorry for not writing for so long. I want to tell you about last weekend. It was Science Rendezvous weekend in Toronto. It is a day in which different scientific institutions, including universities and hospitals, open their doors to the public to talk about science. There were also meetings in coffee shops and pubs where scientists had open conversations about it. I volunteered with the hospital to put a presentation of what we do in the lab. Dave and JP (our technician) helped out and we set up two microscopes to let people look at cells. It was a fun experience to go through the planning and the whole process leading to it, there is so much happening behind the scenes!

    Last night I went to see a play in the theater, Doubt. There was a movie based on it recently. The plot is set in a church school. The principal (a nun) suspects the priest to sexully abuse one of the kids. It is never said but is suggested based on the conversations. She has no proof of it but she is still convinced. The play revolves around the (un)certainty and walks the line between personal conviction, when we believe someone, when we doubt, the demange that can done etc. It was excellent and I recommnad watching the movie.
