    Shana Tova 2020

    September 21, 2020

    COVID changed our usual Jewish New Year tradition, but we still had a great Rosh Hashanah this year.

    We had Miriam, Piotr and the boys over for dinner and enjoyed a lovely hike to Sassafras Point with Danny and Debbie.

    We also enjoyed a Zoom visit with Saba and Savta. Israel is in a second strict lockdown due to COVID cases being very high there.

    We were lucky to have such a beautiful day and lots of outdoor time with Danny and Debbie.

    Wishing for a happy, sweet and healthy New Year for all people, our planet, and its diverse creatures and wonderous environment.
