    Clean Up!

    April 29, 2009

    Last Saturday, spring visited us for one wonderful day.  We celebrated by helping to clean-up trash and broken bottles from our local outdoor climbing location, Rattlesnake Point.  The location we helped to clean had very little trash, but it had a lot of broken glass which needed to be picked up.  This is because teenagers go to the parking lot above to drink and then throw their empty beer bottles over the edge of the cliff.  Luckily we had gloves so picking up glass was easy.  We had a wonderful morning with lots of other climbers, and a general community spirit:

    Looking forward to more beautiful weather soon.  After such a long winter and windy, rainy, cold spring, some sun and warmth will be a blessing.

    Habuk ve neshikot,

    Denise
