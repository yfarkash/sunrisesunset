    Busy and fun weekend

    March 9, 2009

    This was a really busy weekend.

    Friday night we went climbing with our usual group and went to the pub afterward. it was a later night than usual and seem to have set the tone for the weekend. Saturday we woke up and had our usual lazy start but then Denise had to go and clean up some last things out of her apartment. Unfortunately, she had to clean the outside and it was raining . I was at the lab the whole afternoon. We then had dinner plans with friends of Denise who live north of the city, about 1 hour north in a suburb of Toronto called Richmond Hill. Maxin works with Denise in the hospital and him and his wife invited us over for dinner. She plays the guitar and we had some nice fish soup. Since we were that far of the city we decided to spend the night over. We also knew of a climbing gym which is close to them so we planned on trying it out on Sunday morning. Maxin and Elizabeth have two kids so all of us went. Unfortunately, I was up until about 4 in the morning (we changed the clocks one hour forward between Saturday and Sunday) playing music with Elizabth so I was really tired. But it was great waking up again in a house with kids, I miss it. The young girl (Rene) was running around and singing, it was great. We finally made it to the new climbing gym and spent some time with the kids but Denise and I were both too tired so after about 20 minutes on our own we decided to head out.
