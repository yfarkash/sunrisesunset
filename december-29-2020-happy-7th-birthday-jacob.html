December 29, 2020: Happy 7th Birthday Jacob!

December 29, 2020

Our sweet and wonderful boy is growing up so fast- already 7 years old! This is a turning point age. Jacob doesn't seem so little anymore. Tall he is, and he's taking live by storm!

We decorated the table for a special birthday breakfast. The 'theme' of this birthday is rocks and gems, which Jacob loves. In the background, the fun cereals can be spotted - it is a birthday tradition to indulge in the likes of Lucky Charms and Cinnamon Toast Crunch.

During the COVID pandemic and lockdown, standard birthday parties were not an option, so Jacob thought there wouldn't be much that day. Little did he know, we had planned a special surprise Travelling 7th Birthday! There were 7 scrolls prepared to lead us to our party destinations.

Jacob read the first one at our place and learned a bit about the party ahead:

Abba did an amazing job decorating the Honda like a limo (which Jacob loves!). He took out the middle seats, put up lights inside and made it very special!
