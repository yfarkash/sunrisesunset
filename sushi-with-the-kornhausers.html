    Sushi with the Kornhausers

    March 1, 2009

    Last night we had Debbie, Danny, Aviva and David Kornhauser over for a sushi dinner! It was a lot of fun! Yoav and I prepared the ingredients: Chicken, salmon, avocado, sweet potato, cucumber, carrot and many more ingredients so that everyone could roll the sushi together. Instead of rice which is usually used in sushi, we used a very healthy grain called quinoa. It has a lot of protein and we eat it often.
